Why Believe

Why does it matter?

The truth is, without God the conclusion of a meaningless, valueless, purposeless universe is unavoidable. Without meaning, value, and purpose, a happy life consistent with reality is impossible. Many people say life is about "finding your own purpose" and other such nonsense. In reality, doing anything of the sort in a meaningless, valueless, purposeless universe is a practice in self-delusion. If God is real it allows for the possibility of:  meaning — that is our lives could have importance, value — that is right and wrong or good and evil, and purpose — that is there could be some reason for existence. God's existence would allow for a life that is both happy and consistent, not requiring any self-delusion. Abundant life is found in a life abandoned to God.

The good News

Of course, believing in something just because it is a better situation for your life is also self-delusion. The good news is that you don't have to throw away reason or intelligence to believe in God. God and The Bible invite us to explore reality to find Him.

I have not spoken in secret, from somewhere in a land of darkness; I have not said to Jacob's descendants, seek me in vain. I, the Lord, speak the truth; I declare what is right.

Isaiah 45:19

In the following sections we will be taking a cursory glance at the philosophical evidence for God, the scientific evidence for God, and the historical evidence for Jesus. This web page is in no way a complete collection of evidence for God, instead it lays out the main pillars that have helped develop and sustain the most important thing in my life:  A relationship with God.
